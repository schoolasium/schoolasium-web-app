Why Schoolasaium?

Anyone could wonder about new word! The word schoolasium is coined from two words School + Gymnasium. In gymnasium peoples build the body and mussel by physical exercise with the help of many equipments like dumbbells and mussel stretching machines. Burning calories of energy and sweating hours gives a good shape of body. Similarly in Schoolasium child have to develop and nurture their brain through making and developing the toys and equipments hands-on. In the process they have to go though experiment based learning and maker centric learning.

Our Vision

To be a resource centre for schools in Jharkhand and Bihar.

Our Mission

To support low-income private and government schools to cultivate vibrant learning ecosystem where holistic learning is fostered.

Our GOAL

To support the rural schools in Jharkhand and Bihar to establish the STEAM Prayogshala, Makeshala (Tinkering Lab) and Libraries and make MEPS as an EdHub.

Our Objective

Enhance the learning ecosystem in rural Jharkhand & Bihar through implementation of innovative teaching-learning practices.

To establish the STEAM Prayogshala (S- science, T- technology, E- engineering, A- arts, M- Maths), Makeshala and Libraries at schools.

Capacity building of low-cost private and government school administrators and teachers.

To provide educators fellowships to support capacity building of, school teachers, and graduate students and professional who have passion to work on rural education.

To be a part of Skill India initiative of Government of India to equip students and teachers with necessary 21st century skills.

To be a part of maker movement through Atal Innovation Mission (AIM) of Government of India.
